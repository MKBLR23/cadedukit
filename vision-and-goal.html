Vision And GOAL

Our vision is simply to "Provide the next generation with unobstructed personalized, democratic and experienced based education to harness their innate talents and gifts while maximizing their highest potential and raising their social consciousness". Vision is based on the Philosophy that each person is a special tree capable of bearing unique fruits to benefit society. After many false starts, artificial intelligence has taken off using the latest findings in Cognitive Science. Today the machinery question is back with a vengeance, in a new guise. Technologists, economists and philosophers are now debating the implications of artificial intelligence (AI), a fast-moving technology that enables machines to perform tasks that could previously be done only by humans. Its impact could be profound. It threatens workers whose jobs had seemed impossible to automate, from radiologists to legal clerks.

A widely cited study by Carl Benedikt Frey and Michael Osborne of Oxford University, published in 2013, found that 47% of jobs in America were at high risk of being "substituted by computer capital" soon. More recently Bank of America Merrill Lynch predicted that by 2025 the "annual creative disruption impact" from AI could amount to $14 trillion-33 trillion, including a $9 trillion reduction in employment costs thanks to AI-enabled automation of knowledge work; cost reductions of $8 trillion in manufacturing and health care; and $2 trillion in efficiency gains from the deployment of self-driving cars and drones. The McKinsey Global Institute, a thinktank, says AI is contributing to a transformation of society "happening ten times faster and at 300 times the scale, or roughly 3,000 times the impact" of the Industrial Revolution.

With such dyer prediction for our future, our goal inspired by our Philosophy consists of three social scientific components namely Psycho-Analytical to evaluate the individual's Essence Matrix (EM), Computer Science via Autonomous Intelligent Conversational Agents to determine the individual's EM and Educational via the Progressive Education route to recommend the vocational path to a purposeful journey.

  • Leverage Amazon Web Services or Cloud Infrastructure to build a secured, efficient and scalable web based Personal Essence System (PES)
  • Implement Autonomous Intelligent Agents to evaluate results that will assist Institutions of Learning with course recommendation and HR with hiring candidates
  • Combine and implement Briggs Myers and Holland methods (also known as Strong Interest Inventroy) for personality assessment and vocation interest
  • Deploy Learning Management System in Phase II (Ver 2.0) on the AWS Cloud toward Curriculum building
  • Leverage the Progressive Education Principles and curriculum promoted by the likes of John Dewey, Ralph W. Tyler, Paulo Freire and etc.
  • Develop collaboration with Educational Organizations and Institutions to reach this goal
