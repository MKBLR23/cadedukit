OUR PEOPLE

CadEduKit is comprised of a team of energetic and knowledgeable individuals possessing a profound understanding of the application of Information and Communication Technology to industries ranging from banking to education. Coupled with the financial services knowledge of other team members, consultants, and advisors, CadEduKit is well positioned to effectuate the goals it has set for hurting and displaced individuals and emerging technology firms in this hyper-competitive digital marketplace. The paragraphs that follow introduce the core members of the CadEduKit management team and highlight the depth of their experience.
