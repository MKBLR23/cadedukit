OUR COMPANY

Cadet Enterprises Inc (CEI) is a New York State registered Company, founded in 1993. CEI utilizes methods and strategies thoroughly developed in business incubation, technology integration and process improvement. We have 20+ years of extensive and diversified experience in IT consulting and have been dedicated to enterprise business solutions with many successful project implementations at Fortune 50 companies. Since 2001, with the advent of outsourcing and globalization, the world has seen an accelerated shifting of goods, services and capital by International (Mega) Corporations in search of higher efficiency and profit margins while impacting communities, societies throughout the world.

CEI has adapted a Social Entrepreneurship strategy to generate wealth while making long lasting social impact. While social entrepreneurship is not a new phenomenon, the field has experienced enormous growth over the past 20 years, receiving increasing recognition from journalists, philanthropists, researchers, and policymakers as an important and distinctive part of the nation's social, economic, and political landscape. In 2001, CEI conceived an incubation model (nQbate) as a necessary structure to fill a gap: assisting entrepreneurs in depressed communities with great social impact ideas that are not getting enough support and funding to operate a successful venture. CEI had joined with EMBARc.biz which is a business incubation company. This model is more complex and requires the creation of a business development life cycle and facility. I am tackling one node at a time beginning with Knowledge Enablement via the education path using Briggs Meyers (MBTI) and Strong (Holland's Code), Artificial Intelligence and Progressive Education as a cloud based application for the current social challenge to align personality with vocation. Given the advancement in technology and the social problem of re-educating so many displaced workers in our society and worldwide.
